VITacademics

It is a service for students in India manage their academics in college. It helps them keep track of their classes and stay updated on class notices, attendance and marks.

Currently the service is being tested in VIT University, but can potentially be extended to others.

We've highlighted some of the new features we've implemented based on the feedback we've recieved over the past month.

The older version we submitted for Round 1 is still available for reference here.

Full fledged web application

Originally, we used Google App Engine as just a backend server to power our client apps on Android, iOS and Windows Phone.

Now, we built the same experience on the web with a responsive design so students can use the app without having to download it.

Google Signin

We noticed that a lot of institutions use Google Apps for Education to give email IDs to their students, so we adopted Google's OAuth2.0 functionality to provide an easy and secure authentication system for students.

In this way, we can better verify who the student really is, while allowing a single signin across all our platforms.

SMS Support

We added SMS functionality so students without a smartphone can use it too. You can now SMS @vitacademics to query information and recieve updates.

Once you've logged in you can check out our SMS demo here.
